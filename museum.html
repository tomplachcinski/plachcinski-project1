Herbert Bayer: Bauhaus Master | 2023

InDesign | various dimensions

We were given a list of museums in which wee were to browse, in order to find an exhibition that peaked our interest. I chose “Herbert Bayer: Bauhaus Master” from the Cooper Hewitt Design Museum. I've had a big interest in design that came out of The Bauhaus, so I knew this was the exhibition for me. We then had to design a package for the website, similar to one you'd see in a gift shop. Each work shown was designed by Herbert Bayer, but I laid everything out. This package had 5 components.

Component 1: Art Book

The first component in the package is a book showcasing the works in the exhibit, with any information you need to know surrounding the works. It goes into a brief history of Herbert Bayer himself. The cover is inspired by his work on the back cover. Below are a few spreads from this book.

Component 2: Info Book

The second component in the package is a book with any info you may need about the exhibit; ranging from Herbert Bayer's history, how the work came to be, different areas in his work, etc. The cover is inspired by his work on the back cover. Below are a few spreads from this book.

Component 3: Prints

The third component in the package are 4 prints of his work. I decided to go with a Bauhaus-ish look on these prints to really emphasize the theme. 2 are 8.5x11, 2 are 5.5x8x5.

Component 4: Poster

The fourth component in the package is a promotional poster for the exhibit. I took inspiration from one of Herbert Bayer's postcards, one of many he had designed.

Component 5: Donation Card

The fifth component in the package is a donation card I designed for the Cooper Hewitt Design Museum, one similar to the cards you get in gift shops.
