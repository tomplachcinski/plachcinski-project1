WORN | 2023

XD, Illustrator

WORN is a result of countless hours of research and ideation. For my senior capstone, we were given full reign of what we wanted to make, and I knew I wanted to design an app about fashion. On WORN, you can scan barcodes and enter URLs to receive a sustainability report on the brand. You can browse fashion news, and keep up with your friends closets. Alongside the app, I designed a promotional website for the app, as well as a promotional social media carousel.
