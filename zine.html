GorpCore: Fashion of the Outdoors | 2023

InDesign | 5.5in. x 8.5in.

We were tasked with picking a subculture of our choice, and designing a zine around it. Due to my high interest in fashion, I decided to do mine on a subculture in the fashion community, GorpCore. This is basically outdoor, waterproof clothing and accessories.
