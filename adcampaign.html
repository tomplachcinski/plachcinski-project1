Ad Campaign

Break Your Destructive Habits | 2022

Photoshop, Bridge

We we're tasked with picking a social issue among the youth of today, and creating an ad overtiming campaign consisting of 3 posters. The issue I chose was the youth not being able to break away from their devices.

I decided to take pictures of my friends, and manipulate each image, to insert them into dangerous scenarios; a wildfire, a tsunami, and an “apocalypse”. All 3 are still on their devices. This is to represent how no matter how serious the situation, the youth will still be glued to their devices. It can also represent how if you don't pay attention to your surroundings, you might miss something you should've seen.
