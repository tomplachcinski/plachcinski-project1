Surrealist Landscape | 2022

Photoshop

We were tasked with creating our own short narrative as a backstory, then creating a piece in a surrealism style, using image(s) of ourselves and other sourced images for the background. I decided to enlarge myself, reaching down to grab my own self, as that is something I often have odd dreams about.

Backstory:

"The year is 2300. Most of the world is ran by Company X, a nickname for the evil corporation that hides in the shadows. See, the apocalypse has been looming for years and years now, and people are still scared. Historians have been saying it's “in the coming years” for the past 100 years. If it was gonna happen, it wouldve happened already. Taking advantage of all the frightened people, Company X took copies of everyone's DNA for the inevitable apocalypse that's leering on the horizon. However, they didn't take into account that raw DNA unbound by a human vessel mutates at a much faster rate. This opens up a big problem that not even they can contain; giant clones."
