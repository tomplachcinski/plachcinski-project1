Modern Camouflage | 2022

Digital Photography, Photoshop

For this assignment, our teacher allowed us to choose a topic to photograph. Since I have a deep interest in fashion and the “building blocks” of it, I decided to do a material study, seeing what materials I could find that mimic things I found outside.
