Swissted | 2022

Photoshop, InDesign

Inspired by Mike Joyce's “Swissted” book, I decided to redesign concert posters of bands that I enjoy, in Swiss Style design. These were created for a presentation I did on the similarities and differences of “Punk” design and type and Swiss Style.
